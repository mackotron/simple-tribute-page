Mahatma Gandhi

The Incredible Man

Mahatma was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist and political ethicist

    What did he do?

  • Fought for India's independence

  • Pioneered and practiced the principle of Satyagraha—resistance to tyranny through mass nonviolent civil disobedience

  • Fought against racial discrimination in South Africa

  • Lived in South Africa for 21 years

  • Studied law in London

“Be the change that you wish to see in the world” - Mahatma Gandhi
